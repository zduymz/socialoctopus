Social Octopus Society

The Social Octopus Society is a community-driven NFT project which also has some fantastic and unique utility. Our top priorities are bringing everyone together to create the most welcoming community on Solana, and providing our holders with as much value as we can.

STORY

888 small, soft-bodied, eight-limbed, mollusks were happily swimming around the big blue Solana ocean until they were run out of their homes by their predators, The Fishermen. They now seek refuge wherever they can, which is usually in un-safe territories. By minting a Social Octopus, you will give them a safe home in your Solana wallet, where they will hopefully stay for the rest of their lives.

DEEP BLUE DAO

By owning a Social Octopus, you'll get access to the Deep Blue DAO and get to decide the future of the project! Also, 2.5% royalties from secondary sales will go to the community wallet. From there, Social Octopus holders will be able to discuss and vote on what that money should be spent on. Since this is a community-driven project, the community decides everything! Find out more at DeepBlueDAO.io.

EARN $INK

Octopus Ink ($INK) is the SOS utility and (soon to be) governance token! When available, just keep a Social Octopus in your wallet, and you'll receive 10 $INK per week per Octopus!
You'll be able to spend $INK to mint from all future SOS family collections, and later you'll be able to use $INK as a governance token to vote in the Deep Blue DAO! Expect more use-cases for $INK in the future too!
